What is EU Employment Assistance and Who It’s For

MigrantService supports foreign nationals in securing legal employment in EU countries.

We work with verified agencies and directly with employers under official employment contracts.

Main destinations include Germany, the Netherlands, Czech Republic, Poland, and Hungary. The service is available for citizens of any country. Job offers require basic knowledge of English or Russian.

Frequently Asked Questions
About EU Employment

How long does the process take?

Typically between 20 to 30 days, depending on the country.

What documents are required?

Passport, application form, contract, photos, medical certificate — details vary by country.

Do I need to speak the language?

Basic English or Russian is usually enough for most positions.

What kind of guarantee do I get?

You’ll sign an official employment contract. We support you until you start working.

Can I complete everything remotely?

Yes. All paperwork and communication are handled online.

How Much Does EU Employment Assistance Cost?

The cost ranges from €1300 to €2300, depending on the destination and program. Full payment is due before the invitation letter is issued. We do not handle visa applications in your country of residence.
